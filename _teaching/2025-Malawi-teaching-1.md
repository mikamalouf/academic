Introduction to Serological Concepts and Methods
======

The workshop was divided into the following 5 sessions:
1. Introduction to R for serological data analyses
2. Pre-processing serological data
3. Visualizations for serological data
4. Determining serostatus and estimating seroprevalence
5. From serological data to transmission dynamics

Each course was followed by a practical which is available on [GitHub](https://github.com/HopkinsIDD/seroanalytics_workshop).
